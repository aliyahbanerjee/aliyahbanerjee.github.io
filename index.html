Hello! I am Aliyah, a final-year undergraduate economics student in the Department of Economics at UT Austin.

My research interests currently lie in incomplete information games, bargaining, and development (particularly decision-making and bargaining power within rural households, health, and environment).

I am particularly interested in applications of game theory to what I call "intangible development"- measures such as freedom, social status, confidence, and bargaining power in women and girl children that can't or haven't been measured extensively in the field of development.
